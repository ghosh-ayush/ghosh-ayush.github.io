SOCAi Labs: AI Vision for Medical Diagnostics

Built a 0-to-1 AI diagnostics product path centered on PMF, clinical trust, and measurable diagnostic efficiency.

Case StudySOCAi LabsDec 2025 - Present

Context

SOCAi Labs | Dec 2025 - Present

Problem

Dermatology workflows faced slow triage and inconsistent classification quality across geographies and specialist availability.

Decision options

  • Launch broad dermatology coverage from day one.
  • Focus only on model research before productizing workflows.
  • Start with a narrow alopecia-focused MVP and clinician-in-the-loop workflow.

Chosen decision

Started with a narrow alopecia-focused MVP and clinician-in-the-loop workflow.

Tradeoff

Deferred lower-impact features to prioritize model reliability and clinician trust in early releases.

Outcome

  • Established a validated 0-to-1 product direction for AI-assisted hair-loss diagnostics.
  • Improved diagnostic workflow speed and consistency versus manual-only checks in pilot settings.
  • Created a scalable roadmap for clinical deployment and product expansion.

Metric

Improved diagnostic workflow speed and consistency in pilot settings versus manual-only checks.

What I'd do differently

Run payer and clinical integration discovery in parallel earlier to reduce downstream go-to-market and deployment delays.
