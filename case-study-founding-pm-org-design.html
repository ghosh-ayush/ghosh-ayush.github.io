Organizational Design: The Founding PM

Designed the team architecture for a resource-constrained startup by treating organization design as a product scalability problem.

Case StudyStartup Product LeadershipLeadership Case Study

Context

Startup Product Leadership | Leadership Case Study

Problem

Early-stage execution risk increased because generalist-heavy staffing left critical gaps in MLOps and data engineering.

Decision options

  • Scale quickly with mostly generalist hiring.
  • Outsource critical platform and data functions temporarily.
  • Prioritize targeted MLOps and data-engineering roles tied to roadmap risk.

Chosen decision

Prioritized targeted MLOps and data-engineering roles tied directly to roadmap risk.

Tradeoff

Accepted slower broad hiring in exchange for high-leverage specialist roles tied to product risk.

Outcome

  • Closed critical talent gaps threatening product reliability and scale.
  • Improved delivery resilience for end-to-end AI product execution.
  • Created clearer ownership boundaries across product, data, and platform work.

Metric

Closed MLOps and data-engineering capability gaps tied directly to delivery scalability risk.

What I'd do differently

Establish a formal capability matrix and succession risk review in quarter one instead of after initial hiring.
